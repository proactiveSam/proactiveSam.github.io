“Their minds should not abide in form, sound, smell, taste, touch and dharma. Their minds should abide nowhere. “

不应住色生心，不应住声香味触法生心，应生无所住心。 

“If minds abide somewhere, it will be in falsehood.“ 

若心有住，则为非住。
